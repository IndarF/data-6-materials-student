# Data 6 Course Materials

This repository contains student-facing materials for **Data 6: Introduction to Computational Thinking with Data Science and Society**. These materials are based on the Fall 2025 offering of the course and are designed for use by instructors and institutions adopting the Data 6 curriculum.

## For Course Adopters

**Interested in adopting Data 6 at your institution?** Please see our comprehensive [Zero to Data 6: Adoption Guide](https://data6.org/zero-to-data-6/) for step-by-step instructions, technical infrastructure setup, and resources available to adopters.

The adoption guide includes information about:
- Getting access to solutions and private materials
- Setting up technical infrastructure (JupyterHub, grading systems)
- Adapting materials for your institution
- Accessing Canvas templates and grading services

## Course Structure

The course materials follow the structure outlined on the [Data 6 course website](https://data6.org/su25/), which includes:

- **Labs**: Weekly programming assignments introducing key concepts
- **Homeworks**: Problem sets covering course topics
- **Projects**: Larger assignments exploring data science applications
- **Lectures**: Instructional materials and examples

## Repository Contents

- `lab/` - Laboratory assignments (lab01 through lab13)
- `hw/` - Homework assignments (hw01 through hw05)
- `project/` - Project assignments (project1, project2, project_final)
- `lectures/` - Lecture notebooks and materials
